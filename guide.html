HERE IS THE GUIDE !

"Welcome to our game! In order to save your progress between levels,

you'll need to sign in and create an account.

This will allow you to pick up where you left off,

even if you close the game or switch devices.

If you forget your password,

no problem! You can retrieve it by entering your birthday.

So don't forget to sign up and keep your progress safe!"

"To make your way through the game,

you'll need to pass each level one by one.

Each level contains 10 challenging questions that will test your knowledge of the English language.

However, don't worry if you don't score high on a level.

Your score doesn't affect your ability to progress to the next level.

The most important thing is to focus on completing each level's unique challenges and obstacles.

Don't be afraid to experiment and try new things,
as each level will require different skills and strategies.

So keep practicing and honing your skills,

and before you know it, you'll be on your way to completing the game! Good luck!"

"Need some help with the game?

No problem! You have three options for help: First,
I can give you the length of the word you need to guess.

Second, I can tell you the first letter of the word.

And third, I can give you three suggestions to choose from.

However, please note that each help option is only available two times per level,
so use them wisely! Don't rely too heavily on help,
though - the challenge of the game is part of what makes it fun.
So, use your best judgment and good luck!"

"Please note that you have three chances to guess the word in each question.

If you fail to guess the word within these three attempts,

you will automatically move on to the next question.

On the left-hand side of the screen,

you will see a radio button that will turn red if you miss all three chances.

However, if you manage to guess the word correctly within the given attempts,
the radio button will turn green.

Keep this in mind as you play and good luck!"
